## 1. MIAMI REALTORS® / RWorld merger announcement — April 20, 2026

File: `miami-rworld-merger-announcement-2026-04-20.pdf`
URL: https://www.miamirealtors.com/2026/04/20/miami-realtors-and-rworld-announce-merger-creating-the-worlds-largest-local-realtor-association/

Verified facts:

- **93,000 members** — Miami and South Florida REALTORS®, effective May 11, 2026;
  the **largest local Realtor association in the world** (MIAMI 56,000 + RWorld 37,000).
- Membership is **larger than 47 state associations**, **more than double the
  next-largest U.S. local association (43,000)**, and **~33% larger than the next
  largest in the world**.
- **$69 billion** — total real estate volume sold by MIAMI and RWorld members
  in 2025. Attribute to the association network only — never to Carlos
  Uzcategui or United Realty Group.
- Combined MLS (when formed): **third-largest MLS in the nation** (~93,000
  subscribers). Bright MLS (101,000) and CRMLS (99,000) are larger, per T3
  Sixty 2025 rankings. It will be the **largest MLS owned by one U.S. Realtor
  association**. ⚠️ Do **not** claim "largest local MLS in the world" — the
  association's own release ranks it third nationally by subscribers.
- **437+ signed international agreements** with global real estate associations.
- **11 data exchanges** with the largest MLSs in the U.S. and Canada.
- 2,830+ educational seminars/year; 300+ premium marketing tools (member-facing;
  usable if ever needed, sourced here).
- GDX (Global Data Exchange) participation is announced as upcoming — do not
  present as live.
